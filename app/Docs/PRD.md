# Pikar AI - Product Requirements Document

## Executive Summary

Pikar AI is an AI agent orchestration and workflow automation platform designed to optimize business processes across organizations of all sizes—from solopreneurs to enterprise corporations. The platform provides intelligent automation, strategic insights, and operational efficiency through a suite of specialized AI agents that work together to handle everything from content creation to compliance management.

## Product Vision

**Mission**: Empower businesses to achieve more with less by providing intelligent AI agents that automate routine tasks, provide strategic insights, and enable teams to focus on high-value work.

**Vision**: Become the central nervous system for modern businesses, where AI agents seamlessly orchestrate workflows, capture knowledge, and drive strategic decision-making across all business functions.

## Target Users

### 1. Solopreneurs ($99/month)
- **Profile**: Individual entrepreneurs, freelancers, consultants, content creators
- **Pain Points**: Limited time, wearing multiple hats, need for affordable automation
- **Goals**: Streamline operations, maintain consistent brand presence, manage client relationships

### 2. Startups ($297/month)
- **Profile**: Small teams (2-10 people), early-stage companies, growing businesses
- **Pain Points**: Scaling challenges, limited resources, need for team coordination
- **Goals**: Accelerate growth, optimize conversion, establish processes

### 3. SMEs ($597/month)
- **Profile**: Mid-sized businesses (10-50 employees), multiple departments
- **Pain Points**: Cross-department coordination, compliance requirements, vendor management
- **Goals**: Operational excellence, risk mitigation, resource optimization

### 4. Enterprise (Custom Pricing)
- **Profile**: Large corporations (50+ employees), multi-location, complex operations
- **Pain Points**: Global coordination, security requirements, portfolio management
- **Goals**: Strategic command, predictive analytics, white-label solutions

## User Journeys

### Journey 1: Solopreneur Onboarding

**Goal**: Get up and running with AI assistance in under 10 minutes

1. **Sign Up**: Create account with email or Google OAuth
2. **Business Profile**: Enter business name, industry, and goals
3. **Agent Selection**: Choose 3 core agents (pre-selected: Executive, Marketing, Content)
4. **Brand Setup**: Upload logo, define brand voice and colors
5. **First Task**: Create first social media post with AI assistance
6. **Calendar Setup**: Connect social accounts and schedule first post
7. **Dashboard Tour**: Interactive walkthrough of key features
8. **Success**: First post scheduled, dashboard populated with demo data

**Key Experience**: Simple, guided, immediate value

### Journey 2: Startup Team Collaboration

**Goal**: Coordinate a product launch across marketing, sales, and support teams

1. **Initiative Creation**: Create "Q2 Product Launch" initiative
2. **Team Invitation**: Invite 5 team members with role assignments
3. **Workflow Design**: Build launch workflow with approval gates
   - Marketing creates campaign (Content Agent)
   - Sales reviews messaging (Sales Intelligence Agent)
   - Support prepares knowledge base (Customer Support Agent)
4. **Agent Orchestration**: Configure agents to work together
5. **Execution**: Launch workflow, monitor progress in real-time
6. **Brain Dump**: Team captures insights and learnings via voice notes
7. **Analytics Review**: Measure campaign performance and ROI
8. **Optimization**: AI suggests improvements for next launch

**Key Experience**: Collaborative, transparent, data-driven

### Journey 3: SME Compliance Management

**Goal**: Achieve 95%+ compliance automation across departments

1. **Compliance Audit**: Run initial assessment of current state
2. **Policy Upload**: Import existing policies and procedures
3. **Rule Configuration**: Set up automated compliance rules
4. **Department Assignment**: Assign policies to relevant teams
5. **Vendor Management**: Add vendors and track performance
6. **Risk Assessment**: Identify and prioritize business risks
7. **Automated Monitoring**: Compliance Agent monitors activities
8. **Violation Handling**: Escalation queue routes issues to reviewers
9. **Reporting**: Generate compliance reports for stakeholders
10. **Continuous Improvement**: Track compliance score over time

**Key Experience**: Proactive, automated, audit-ready

### Journey 4: Enterprise Portfolio Management

**Goal**: Gain strategic visibility across global business units

1. **Multi-Business Setup**: Add 10+ business entities to portfolio
2. **Data Warehouse**: Configure ETL pipelines from each business
3. **Strategic Dashboard**: Customize global command center view
4. **Predictive Analytics**: Enable AI forecasting across portfolio
5. **Cross-Business Workflows**: Create workflows spanning entities
6. **Security Configuration**: Set up SSO, SCIM, and KMS
7. **White-Label Deployment**: Brand platform for internal use
8. **Executive Briefing**: AI generates weekly strategic summary
9. **Scenario Planning**: Model different strategic directions
10. **Decision Making**: Use AI insights to allocate resources

**Key Experience**: Strategic, comprehensive, predictive

## User Experience Principles

### 1. Progressive Disclosure
- Show basic features first, reveal advanced capabilities as users grow
- Tier-appropriate feature visibility (no overwhelming enterprise features for solopreneurs)
- Contextual help and tooltips throughout the interface

### 2. AI-First Interactions
- Natural language inputs wherever possible
- Proactive suggestions and recommendations
- Conversational interfaces for complex tasks

### 3. Visual Clarity
- Clean, modern design with consistent patterns
- Data visualization for complex information
- Status indicators and progress tracking

### 4. Mobile Responsiveness
- Full functionality on tablet and desktop
- Optimized layouts for different screen sizes
- Touch-friendly controls

### 5. Performance & Speed
- Real-time updates without page refreshes
- Instant search and filtering
- Background processing for long-running tasks
